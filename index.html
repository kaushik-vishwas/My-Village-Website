About Village


According to Cencus 2011 and many Government Databases, India consists 650000+ villages which the most villages in all over the world in number. And around 70% population of India belongs to rural area. So villages are the backbone of India. Gutri is one of the villages of India. . Gutri village is located in Gaighata subdivision of North Twenty Four Parganas district in West Bengal,
Geopraphy -
The total geographical area of village is 240.2 hectares.
Population -
Gutri has a total population of 2,141 peoples, out of which male population is 1,057 while female population is 1,084. There are about 533 houses in gutri village.
Literacy Rate -
Literacy rate of gutri village is 74.82% out of which 80.70% males and 69.10% females are literate
